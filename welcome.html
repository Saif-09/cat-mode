You're set — Cat Mode 🐾

Block your keyboard & trackpad so a pet can't disrupt your video. Two quick steps:

  1. Pin the extension Click the puzzle-piece icon in the toolbar and pin Cat Mode so the on/off toggle is one click away.
  2. Set your global shortcut Chrome doesn't always assign the suggested Ctrl/⌘ + Shift + 9 automatically. Open the shortcuts page and assign one so you can toggle Cat Mode from anywhere.
  3. Turn it on Click the toolbar icon and flip Block keyboard & trackpad. Unlock with Ctrl + Shift + K (editable in the popup).

The toolbar toggle always works — the global shortcut is just a convenience.
